1. Introduction

Maps for Clash ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Maps for Clash mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

Important Disclaimer: This is an unofficial fan-made app. Clash of Clans is a trademark of Supercell Oy. This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Supercell.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Email Address: When you create an account or sign in
  • Display Name/Username: Your chosen username for chat and community features
  • Profile Information: Any information you provide in your profile

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, timestamps, crash reports

2.3 Data from Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Email, user ID, authentication tokens
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Chat messages, bookmarks, user preferences
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Advertising ID, device information, ad interaction data
  • Supabase: Chat data, real-time messaging information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Account Management: Create and manage user accounts
  • App Functionality: Provide core features (bookmarks, chat, news, videos)
  • Personalization: Save preferences and customize your experience
  • Communication: Send notifications about app updates and features

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Analytics: Understand app usage and improve features
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Locations

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: User authentication and database
  • Supabase: Chat messages and real-time data
  • Local Device: Cached data, bookmarks, preferences

4.2 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication
Note: While we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Advertising and AdMob

6.1 How Ads Work

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, user-resettable ID for advertising
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, screen size
  • Ad Interactions: Which ads you view or click
  • General Location: Country/region (not precise location)

6.2 Your Ad Choices

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID

6.3 Ad Types in Our App

  • Banner Ads (bottom of screen)
  • Interstitial Ads (between content)
  • Rewarded Ads (optional, for in-app rewards)
  • Native Ads (in content feeds)
Important: We do NOT sell your personal information to advertisers. AdMob uses aggregated data for ad targeting.

7.1 COPPA Compliance

8. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

8.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update: Modify your profile and account information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format

8.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

  1. In-App: Settings → Account → Manage Data
  2. Email: Contact us at clashofbases.dev@gmail.com
  3. Response Time: We will respond within 30 days

8.3 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

8.4 CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Chat Messages: Retained for app functionality
  • Bookmarks: Retained until manually deleted
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for up to 26 months

9.1 Deletion Process

When you delete your account:

  1. Personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  2. Chat messages may be anonymized (username removed)
  3. Some data may be retained for legal/security purposes

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country:

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses (EU) and compliance with applicable laws.
